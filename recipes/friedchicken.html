Fried Chicken Recipe

I enjoy fried chicken because it tastes good and the best place to get fast food fried chicken if you don't want to make it is popeyes. If you do want to make it here's a recipe that may or may not be good.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Cut up chicken
  2. Put flour in large plastic bag and add seasonings of choice
  3. Dip chicken in buttermilk
  4. Place chicken on cooking tray and let it rest until flour is paste-like
  5. Fill pot with oil and turn on heat until very hot
  6. Place chicken inside pot and turn down heat when browning is acheived
  7. After 30 minutes raise heat until chicken is crispy
  8. Drain oil and serve
