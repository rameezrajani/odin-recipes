Chicken Alfredo Recipe

I don't know how to make chicken alfredo. This webpage claims to. So try it out if you want to make some chicken alfredo pasta.

Ingredients

  • Package Penne Pasta
  • Cup Butter
  • Garlic
  • Cream cheese
  • Milk
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Dried Parsley
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Bring large pot of water to boil and add penne to cook it for 11 minutes
  2. Melt butter in pan over medium heat. Add garlic and until golden. Add in cream cheese and milk, stir until smooth and low the heat. Add Parmesan cheese, parsley, Italian seasoning,salt, and black pepper. Continue stirring until sauce is smooth.
  3. Season chicken and cook in large skillet. After cooked cut chicken into half inch cubes.
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  5. Layer pasta chicken and sauce in casserole dish. Sprinkle top with parmesan cheese and cover with foil.
  6. Bake dish in preheaded oven for about 15 minutes and serve.
