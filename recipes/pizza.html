Homemade Pizza Recipe

Pizza is an italian dish that is very popular around the world and with my stomach. I like pizza more than I like squidward. Here's a recipe that might be good or not. I don't really know I never tried it so it may or may not be good.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F
  2. Mix warm water, yeast, and sugar in bowl and let it rest for 10 minutes
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to the mixture
  4. Beat the mixture until smooth and let it rest for 5 minutes
  5. Roll dough out into 12 inch circle
  6. Transfer dough to pan
  7. Add sauce and topping of choice
  8. Bake pizza in preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes until golden brown
